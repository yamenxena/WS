## Design Direction

Per Majaz's differentiation — "every project starts with a design narrative, not a floor plan" [SOURCE: context/strategy.md]:

**Narrative:** The house as a **framed landscape** — each room is a curated view of garden, pool, or sky. The courtyard is the heart, bringing light deep into the plan while maintaining privacy.

**Key Moves:**
1. Central courtyard separating public (ground) from private (first floor)
2. Double-height living space connecting to courtyard
3. Master suite with private terrace overlooking pool
4. Recessed entrance creating arrival compression → release into courtyard
