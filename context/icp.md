# Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

## Demographics
- **Who:** High-net-worth individuals, families, or investors
- **Location:** Abu Dhabi (Saadiyat, Reem Island, Al Shamkha, Khalifa City, Yas Island)
- **Budget:** AED 2M+ construction budget (typically AED 3-8M for luxury villas)

## Project Type
- Bespoke modern villas (300-800 sqm built-up area)
- New construction on owned land
- Emphasis on conceptual, contemporary, or modern Arabic design
- Premium finishes and landscape integration

## Psychographics
- Values design quality over speed
- Appreciates conceptual storytelling (not just floor plans)
- Wants a single point of contact from concept to handover
- Expects bilingual communication (AR/EN)
- Referral-driven (word of mouth, Instagram discovery)

## ICP Scoring Criteria (1-5)

| Criterion | Weight | Score 5 (Perfect) | Score 1 (No Match) |
|-----------|:------:|-------------------|-------------------|
| Budget | 30% | AED 3M+ villa | <AED 1M or commercial |
| Location | 25% | Abu Dhabi premium zone | Outside UAE |
| Project Type | 25% | Bespoke villa, new build | Renovation or commercial |
| Engagement | 20% | Design-focused, full-service | Price-only, partial scope |

**Score ≥ 4:** Pursue actively  
**Score 3:** Qualify further  
**Score ≤ 2:** Politely decline or refer
