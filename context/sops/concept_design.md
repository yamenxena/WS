# SOP: Concept Design

## Scope
From client brief through concept approval. Does NOT extend to working drawings or municipality submission.

## Process

### Step 1: Client Brief
- Meet client (in-person or video)
- Document: site location, plot size, built-up area target, budget range
- Document: design preferences (modern, contemporary, modern Arabic, minimalist)
- Document: room program (bedrooms, living areas, service areas, outdoor)
- Deliverable: `Brief Document` (1-2 pages)

### Step 2: Site Analysis
- Obtain plot coordinates and survey
- Check setback requirements (Abu Dhabi Municipality standards)
- Analyze orientation (sun path, prevailing wind, views)
- Identify constraints (easements, adjacent buildings, access)
- Deliverable: `Site Analysis Summary`

### Step 3: Concept Sketches
- Develop 2-3 massing options (hand sketch or digital)
- Consider: privacy, natural light, ventilation, indoor-outdoor flow
- Present options to client with narrative explanation
- Deliverable: `Concept Options` (sketches + narrative)

### Step 4: 3D Visualization
- Develop selected option into 3D model
- Produce exterior renders (2-3 views minimum)
- Produce interior mood boards for key spaces
- Deliverable: `3D Visualization Package`

### Step 5: Client Review & Revision
- Present 3D package to client
- Document feedback and revision requests
- Maximum 2 revision rounds included in base fee
- Additional revisions billed separately
- Deliverable: `Approved Concept Design`

### Step 6: Handoff
- Package approved concept for architecture & engineering phase
- Update project status in `operations/projects.md`
- Archive concept files

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Brief captures all client requirements
- [ ] Setbacks comply with municipality standards
- [ ] 3D renders match approved concept
- [ ] Client sign-off documented
