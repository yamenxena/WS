# SOP: Site Supervision

## Scope
Construction supervision from permit issuance through completion certificate. Covers structural, MEP, finishing, landscape, and safety.

## Inspection Categories

### 1. Structural
- Foundation (excavation, rebar, formwork, concrete pour)
- Columns and beams (rebar spacing, concrete grade, formwork alignment)
- Slabs (rebar mesh, conduit placement, concrete pour)
- Waterproofing (membrane, drainage)

### 2. MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)
- Electrical: conduit runs, panel locations, wiring
- Plumbing: pipe runs, drainage slopes, water pressure
- HVAC: duct runs, unit placement, refrigerant lines
- Fire safety: sprinkler layout, fire alarm, smoke detection

### 3. Finishing
- Blockwork and plastering
- Tiling and flooring
- Painting and wall finishes
- Joinery and woodwork
- Kitchen and bathroom fixtures

### 4. Landscape
- Hardscape (paving, walls, pool)
- Softscape (planting, irrigation)
- External lighting
- Boundary walls and gates

### 5. Safety
- PPE compliance on site
- Fall protection
- Material storage
- Fire extinguisher locations
- First aid availability

## Report Structure

Each supervision visit produces a report with:
1. **Header:** date, project, inspector, visit number
2. **Category:** which inspection category (from above)
3. **Findings:** item-by-item observations
4. **Severity:** Critical / Major / Minor / Observation
5. **Photo Ref:** GPS-timestamped photo reference (2026 standard)
6. **Actions:** required corrections with deadline
7. **Status:** Open / In Progress / Closed

## Frequency
- Structural phase: 2-3 visits/week
- MEP rough-in: 1-2 visits/week
- Finishing: 1 visit/week
- Pre-handover: daily for final punch list
