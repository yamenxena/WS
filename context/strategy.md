# Majaz — Business Strategy

## Vision
To be the UAE's most sought-after boutique architecture firm for bespoke luxury villas, known for conceptual design excellence, seamless municipality approvals, and meticulous site supervision — operational in both Abu Dhabi and Dubai.

## Current Reality (Honest Assessment)
- **Strength:** Waseem's design talent, municipality knowledge (AD), client trust
- **Weakness:** One-person bottleneck. Waseem handles EVERYTHING: design, municipality, supervision, client calls, invoicing, content, leads
- **Opportunity:** Dubai market entry (Villa Jumeirah), delegation to junior staff, AI-assisted operations
- **Threat:** Communication latency losing leads; cash flow pressure from overdue invoices; burnout risk

## Differentiation
1. **Conceptual depth** — every project starts with a design narrative, not a floor plan
2. **Municipality mastery** — Abu Dhabi streamlined approvals (learning Dubai process)
3. **Bilingual excellence** — full AR/EN capability in all communications
4. **End-to-end ownership** — concept through construction supervision to handover
5. **NEW: Dubai + Abu Dhabi** — dual-city capability (unique for boutique firm)

## Service Lines
| Service | Phase | Revenue Model | Current Load |
|---------|-------|--------------|:------------:|
| Concept Design | Brief → 3D → approval | Fixed fee per project | 2 active ⚠️ |
| Architecture & Engineering | Working drawings + MEP | % of construction cost | 1 active |
| Municipality Approvals | Submission → clearance | Included in A&E fee | 2 pending |
| Site Supervision | Construction → handover | Monthly retainer | 1 active ⚠️ |

## Target Market
- High-net-worth individuals in Abu Dhabi AND Dubai
- Bespoke modern villas (300-800 sqm built-up)
- **Abu Dhabi:** Saadiyat, Reem Island, Yas Island, Khalifa City, Al Shamkha
- **Dubai (NEW):** Jumeirah, Emirates Hills, Al Barsha, Dubai Hills

## Competitive Advantage in Dubai
- Most Abu Dhabi firms don't serve Dubai (different municipality, different codes)
- Most Dubai firms are too large for bespoke single villas
- Majaz bridges both markets with boutique attention

## Fee Structure
| Project Type | Fee Range (AED) | Payment Terms |
|-------------|:--------------:|--------------|
| Villa (500-800 sqm) | 250K-500K | 30% upfront, 30% at municipality, 40% at handover |
| Townhouse | 120K-200K | 30% upfront, 30% at drawings, 40% at submission |
| Supervision only | 8K-15K/month | Monthly retainer |

## Marketing Strategy
- Instagram: project showcases, behind-the-scenes, 3D renders
- LinkedIn: professional network, municipality insights, Dubai expansion story
- Referrals: existing client satisfaction drives word-of-mouth (strongest channel — 60% of leads)
