# Content Post Workflow

## Steps

1. **READ** `context/GOALS.md` — align post with quarterly marketing goal
2. **READ** `context/strategy.md` — match firm voice and differentiation
3. **READ** `comms/whatsapp_samples.md` or `comms/email_samples.md` — match AR/EN tone
4. **DRAFT** post:
   - Platform: Instagram / LinkedIn (specify)
   - Language: AR / EN / bilingual
   - Tone: professional, conceptual, premium (from strategy)
   - Hashtags + call to action
5. **WRITE** entry to `operations/content_pipeline.md` — add to calendar
6. **WRITE** draft to `output/communications/YYYY-MM-DD_Post_[Topic].md`
   - Include `[SOURCE: context/strategy.md]` for brand voice
   - Include `[AUTH:]` footer
7. **LOG** append to `operations/logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## AP Guards
- AP13: never mention client names in public content without explicit approval
- AP68: cite `[SOURCE:]` for brand claims
